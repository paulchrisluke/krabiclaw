# Tenant Compliance as Platform Data

Legal and professional-service tenants need disclaimers, entity/nonprofit metadata, policy links, and legal document references, but these concepts are not owned by a single template. KrabiClaw will model tenant compliance as shared platform data rendered by templates, with full URL-bearing policy and notice content stored as tenant pages and legal files served through the platform media/file system.

## Considered Options

- Generic tenant compliance settings plus tenant pages: reusable across regulated tenants and keeps templates presentation-only.
- Legal-template fields: fast for one template, but hides compliance data inside rendering code.
- Vertical-specific legal fields: clearer for legal, but too narrow for reusable professional-service compliance data.

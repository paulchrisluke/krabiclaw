# Flexible Pricing Pages and Optional Calculators

Professional-service tenants may need public pricing pages and reviewed calculators, but donations do not need native platform handling for the NCLS cutover. KrabiClaw will support flexible pricing-page content and optional configured calculator components, while treating donation buttons as external links for now and avoiding arbitrary custom calculator scripts.

## Considered Options

- Flexible pricing page plus optional calculator component: supports NCLS needs while keeping the behavior reviewable and reusable.
- External links and static pricing only: safer, but underserves NCLS pricing/calculator requirements.
- NCLS custom calculator code: fastest for one tenant, but creates hidden client-specific platform debt.

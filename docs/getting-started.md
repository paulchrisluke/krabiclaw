# Getting Started

Welcome to KrabiClaw! This guide will help you get started with building your restaurant website.

## Sign Up

1. Go to [krabiclaw.com](https://krabiclaw.com)
2. Click "Start free"
3. Sign up with Google or create an account
4. Complete your profile

## Create Your Site

1. Choose a template
2. Enter your restaurant name
3. Select your theme
4. Connect your Google Business Profile (optional but recommended)

## Add Your Menu

1. Go to the Menu section
2. Add menu categories
3. Add menu items with prices
4. Upload photos of your dishes
5. Publish your menu

## Customize Your Site

1. Update your restaurant information
2. Add your location details
3. Upload photos
4. Customize colors and fonts
5. Set up your domain

## Launch

1. Preview your site
2. Check all pages
3. Connect your custom domain (optional)
4. Launch your site!
